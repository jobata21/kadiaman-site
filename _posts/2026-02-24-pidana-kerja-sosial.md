### Executive Summary
An analysis of the paradigm shift in Indonesian sentencing—moving from purely retributive incarceration toward restorative justice through social work. This assessment highlights how urban centers can utilize community service to address public resource gaps.

### Strategic Key Points
* **Paradigm Shift:** Transformation from prison-centric solutions to social contribution.
* **Urban Efficiency:** Strategic deployment of community service labor for infrastructure.
* **Modern Jurisprudence:** Implementing the new Criminal Code (KUHP) within the context of penal effectiveness.
