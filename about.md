# Joseph Jubata Kadiaman
**Principal Strategic Advisor**

Expertise in **Military Law (LOAC/IHL)**, **Geopolitical Strategy**, and **Macro Risk Analysis**. 

I provide high-stakes advisory for decision-makers who operate in environments where legal frameworks intersect with national security and institutional power. My approach is rooted in **Realpolitik**—understanding that law is often used as a weaponized strategy (*Lawfare*) to achieve tactical and strategic objectives.

### Professional Focus
* **Defense Doctrine:** Integration of legal norms into operational military frameworks.
* **Sovereign Compliance:** Ensuring institutional stability within complex international regulations.
* **Risk Decoupling:** Identifying and mitigating asymmetric legal risks before they escalate into systemic crises.
